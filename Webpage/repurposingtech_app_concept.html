Internet Buddy(App Concept)

Giving you the knowledge to understand and maintain the data on your device.

Control the data that apps are allowed to use.

Get notified whenever apps use your data and explore resources to assist your data mediation.

Utilize explanations and suggestions to lower Data Score.

What does this app do?

This app allows you to view and mediate the data that other applications use. While a full tech detox isn't on everyone's mind, we can provide you with the knowledge and choice to better understand when and how your data is accessed.

What does this mean for you?

While the app does use the user's data, the user is able to define the applications range of accessibility to their data. This allows you to be in control of your involvement within Data Mediation. The goal to is invite the user to learn and become more aware of how their phone sends data to the applications installed on the device.

Controlling your Data Score

The user gets notified every time the application detects data transfers to other applications. Each notification will provide a small message informing you of the information sent and the data's context within the requested application. Upon interacting with the notification, the app provides a detailed description of the data sent and a list of suggestions to help prevent future involvment. The app does also provide the user with insight of how the suggestion can alter their overall user experience with sed application. The more the user interacts with the recommendations, the lower their Data Score becomes, giving the user a voluntary incentive to mediate their data.
